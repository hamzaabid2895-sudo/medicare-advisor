Understanding Medicare

Medicare Parts A, B, C, and D — Simply Explained

Medicare can feel like alphabet soup. Here's what each part actually means for your health and your wallet.

A

Hospital Coverage

Covers inpatient stays, nursing care, etc.

B

Medical Coverage

Doctor visits, outpatient, and preventive care.

C

Medicare Advantage

Private plans that bundle A and B with drug/extra benefits.

D

Drug Coverage

Covers prescription medications selectively.
